About Taizhou Try Medical Supplies

Professional manufacturer and exporter of medical consumables established in 2002, with over 20 years of experience in the healthcare industry.

We specialize in producing high-quality medical dressing packs, gauze pads, surgical packs, gauze balls, medical bandages and other disposable medical supplies that meet international standards including ISO 13485 certification.

Our state-of-the-art facilities ensure consistent quality and reliable delivery to global markets.

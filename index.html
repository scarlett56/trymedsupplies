About Taizhou Try Medical Supplies

Taizhou Try Medical Supplies Company, established in 2002 and located in the Baili Industrial Zone of Taizhou City, Jiangsu Province, is a professional manufacturer of medical consumables, integrating R&D, production, and sales.

We specialize in dressing kits, gauze swabs, surgical packs, gauze balls, medical bandages, and other disposable products. Equipped with advanced production facilities, sterile workshops, and an ISO 13485-certified quality management system, we ensure our products are safe, reliable, and of the highest quality.
